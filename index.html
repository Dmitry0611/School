Why should you choose us?

There are many courses and programs for any interest of the child

More than 50 courses and programs on programming, drawing, robotics, editing,
animation, web design. If the child is a football player, he can make a project on a
football theme, a guitarist – on a musical one.

Game form of classes and informal atmosphere

The teacher conducts the lesson in real time, gives instant feedback and
engages in the subject through practical lessons on interactive platforms
where students create their own projects.

New acquaintances

The child will not only get to know cool professionals in the field of IT technologies,
but also with their peers from other cities and countries.

Convenience, safety and comfort

Choose a convenient format and time for classes. Comfortable online lessons from
home and no worries due to the late return of the child home.

Free career guidance

The methodologist will talk about modern technologies and professions, determine
the level of knowledge, make an individual training program.
